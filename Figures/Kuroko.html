Kuroko Tetsuya 1/8 Scale

Manufacturer: MegaHouse

Retail Price: 7600 Yen

Release Date: 2014

I found him brand new on Mandarake for a good price so I got him to put into sports anime characters shelf :D. Great quality from MegaHouse despite it's age. He also doesn't have a support pole unlike a lot of the other Kuroko' Basketball figures, which was more pleasing to the eye for me. When I opened him I noticed that he felt sticky due to being in a box for a while, but eventually with time and tissues, he doesn't feel this way anymore.
