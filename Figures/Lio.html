Lio Fotia 1/7 Scale

Manufacturer: Kotobukiya

Retail Price: 11800 Yen

Release Date: 2020

My very first scale figure and still one of my favourites to this date. He has a great pose and the colourful and unique base elevates the figure to the next level. My only gripe with it is that his hair has a noticeable seamline, but I do understand that it needs to there so that you can switch out faceplates. Though I think it could be more well hidden.
