Revoltech Danboard Mini

Version: Cheero

Manufacturer: Kaiyodo

Retail Price: 1104 Yen

Release Date: 2014

I got this figure years back, probably when I was in elementary school. It's very cute and I like that its eyes can light up. The main issue with it (at least in my copy) is that the peg on the base doesn't fit into the hole at the bottom of figure's foot, making it prone to falling over sometimes.
