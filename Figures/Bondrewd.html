Bondrewd Non-Scale Figure

Manufacturer: Ques Q

Retail Price: 29800 Yen

Release Date: 2023 (Tentative)

I like Made in Abyss, but I didn't really feel like getting any figures from that series until this one. Bondrewd is my favourite character from Made in Abyss and his figure is the most unique looking one that I will have currently. Judging at the prototype, I think Ques Q has done a great job at his scuplt and paintjob despite him being mostly black. He's definitely pricey and is my most expensive figure to date, but at the very least he has a light up feature and his backpack can be opened up so that you can put the cartridge accessory inside.
