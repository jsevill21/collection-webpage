Tomioka Giyuu 1/8 Scale

Manufacturer: Alter

Retail Price: 20800 Yen

Release Date: 2023 (Tentative)

I wanted a Giyu figure and I glad I decided to wait for this one. Once again Alter did a fantastic job with the sculpt and paintjob based on the prototype. I do think he's a bit expensive considering he's a 1/8, but at least it isn't as bad as what other figures are going for nowadays. Demon slayer figures typically go down in price in the aftermarket, but I decided that I was too impatient to wait for (predicted) his drop in price and pre-ordered anyway.
